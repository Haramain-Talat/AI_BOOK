# Introduction

Welcome to the comprehensive textbook on Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics. This textbook covers the principles and applications of physical AI and humanoid robotics, providing a thorough understanding of the field.

## Learning Outcomes

After studying this textbook, you should be able to:
- Understand the fundamental concepts of physical AI
- Analyze the design and control of humanoid robots
- Apply AI techniques to robotic systems
- Evaluate the ethical implications of humanoid robotics

## Overview

This textbook is organized into several chapters covering different aspects of physical AI and humanoid robotics:

1. Introduction to Physical AI
2. Fundamentals of Humanoid Robotics
3. Perception Systems for Physical AI
4. Control Systems for Humanoid Robots
5. Learning and Adaptation in Physical AI
6. Applications and Ethics of Humanoid Robotics

Each chapter contains detailed explanations, practical examples, and exercises to reinforce your learning.
